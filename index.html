Food Resources for DFW and Beyond

Food Insecurity

The inability to afford healthy food for all family members has had a substantial increase during the covid pandemic due to umeployment. This has always been an issue of income inequality. In an effort to address the needs of a growing hungry population our team has gathered information and resources related to where to get food, how to access food related services, growing food through gardening, and cooking on a budget.

This project was created as a part of Hack the Culture in an effort to be a part of change for the good of all people.
